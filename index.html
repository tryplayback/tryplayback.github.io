        What is it
        
A quiet screen that does one thing — beautifully.

        

          Playback connects to your phone, laptop or speaker over AirPlay, Bluetooth or cable,
          and mirrors whatever's playing as a calm, glanceable display. No feeds. No notifications.
          Just the music, in view.
